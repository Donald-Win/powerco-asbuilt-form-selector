# Powerco As-Built Form Selector

**Quick reference tool for finding required as-built forms and commissioning certificates**

---

## What This App Does

This app helps you quickly identify which forms and certificates you need to complete for your electrical work, based on the 360S014 Electricity As-Built Reporting Standard (Version 12, July 2025).

Simply select the type of work you're doing, and the app shows you:
- ✅ Required as-built documentation forms
- ✅ Required commissioning & test certificates
- 📄 Direct download links to all forms

---

## How to Use

### Step 1: Select Your Work Type

Use the search box or scroll through the list to find your type of work:
- LV Service Connection
- HV Distribution Network
- Transformers
- Subdivisions
- Switchgear
- And 16 more work types...

### Step 2: View Required Forms

Once you select a work type, you'll see:

**Required Forms** (blue cards)
- These are the as-built documentation forms you must complete
- Each form has a download button
- Tap any form to open the PDF

**Commissioning & Test Certificates** (green section)
- Tap to expand this section
- These verify your equipment is safe and ready for service
- Submit these separately from your as-built forms

### Step 3: Download What You Need

- **Tap any form** to open and view the PDF
- **Download to your device** for offline access
- **Print directly** from the PDF viewer

## Understanding the Forms

### As-Built Documentation Forms (360S014 Series)

These document what was built and must be submitted within **15 working days** of completion.

**Common forms include:**
- **360S014EA** - Low Voltage Connection Record
- **360S014EB** - Electrical Distribution Record
- **360S014EC** - Pole Record
- **360S014EE** - Electrical Equipment Record
- **360S014EG** - Transformer Record
- **360S014EJ** - Earth Installation and Test Record

### Commissioning & Test Certificates (220F028 Series)

These verify equipment is safe and ready for service. Complete these **before** energizing equipment.

**Common certificates include:**
- **220F028A** - Pre-Commissioning HV Inspection Certificate
- **220F028B** - Distribution Transformer Commissioning Certificate
- **220F028E** - LV Customer Connection and Polarity Checks
- **220F028G** - Underground LV Distribution Circuit Test Certificate

## Special Notes

### 📝 Important Reminders

- **Multiple forms may be required** depending on the complexity of your work
- **Pre-commissioning and commissioning certificates** should be uploaded separately from your as-built forms
- Forms with **"or Form"** notes mean you can use either the specified form OR an alternative (like Equipment Record Card)
- Some work types have **special requirements** - read the notes shown for each work type

### 🔍 Form Notes Explained

When you select a work type, you may see notes like:

- **"For UG Distribution Network"** - This form only applies to underground work
- **"Equip Record Card or Form"** - You can use either format
- **"For Critical Spares"** - Only required for critical spare equipment
- **"Where an Earth Test has been taken"** - Only if you performed earth testing

## Tips for Field Use

### ✅ Best Practices

1. **Check requirements before starting work** - Know what forms you'll need
2. **Download forms to your device** - Have them ready even without signal
3. **Take photos as you go** - Many forms require photographic evidence
4. **Complete forms daily** - Don't wait until the end of the project
5. **Submit within 15 working days** - This is a Powerco requirement

### 📱 Mobile-Friendly Features

- **Large tap targets** - Easy to use with gloves
- **Clear text** - Readable even in bright sunlight
- **Fast search** - Find your work type quickly
- **No typing required** - Just tap and select

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario: Installing a New Transformer

1. Select **"Transformers - Overhead"** or **"Transformers - Ground mount"**
2. You'll need these as-built forms:
   - Pole Record (if overhead)
   - Transformer Record
   - Equipment Record Card
   - Electrical Equipment Record
   - Earth Installation and Test Record
   - Manufacturer Test Certificates
3. Plus these commissioning certificates:
   - Pre-Commissioning HV Inspection Certificate
   - Distribution Transformer Commissioning Certificate

### Scenario: New Underground Subdivision

1. Select **"Subdivisions - Underground"**
2. You'll need 8 as-built forms plus 4 commissioning certificates
3. Note: "A plan detailing all cable details is allowed" as an alternative

### Scenario: Simple Service Connection

1. Select **"LV Service Connection - OH and UG"**
2. You'll only need:
   - One as-built form (360S014EA)
   - One commissioning certificate (220F028E)
